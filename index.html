New to game development?

If you're new to game development, our workshops and online articles will help you get started. Whether you're an artist or a programmer, we have a complete set of articles neatly organized as skill trees that help clear up important concepts. These are designed to complement and not replace established tutorials by industry experts.

Check out what we've made

Over the last year we've conducted game jams and supported various projects. You can find all of them on our Itch page. Most of our projects are open source* with liberal licenses, so that you can treat them as a reference to learn too.
